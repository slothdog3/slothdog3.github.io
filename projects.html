Custom Laser Cavity Mode Simulation GUI in MATLAB (UR)

Created a MATLAB GUI that checks stability of cavity and for stable cavities simulates allowed transverse and longitudinal laser modes based on user input of cavity length, end mirror size, gain bandwidth of laser medium, etc. Allows ability to toggle between Hermite-Gaussian and Laguerre-Gaussian TEM modes or show the superposition of the allowed TEM modes.
